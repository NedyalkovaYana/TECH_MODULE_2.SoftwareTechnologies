Why Choosing the Right Storage Infrastructure.

All-Flash Array as a DevOps enabler Historically, one of the reasons that Dev and Ops environments were siloed was because the underlying infrastructure – and storage in particular – did not have the horsepower to host both of these environments on same platform. This led to Dev and QA teams making their own redundant copies of datasets. Imagine if you had a 10TB database that gets copied around. . . you get the picture. Using 3PAR All-flash array, Dev and Ops can be consolidated on the same storage platform. While DevOps is a power enabler that promotes collaboration between typically siloed Dev and Ops teams, it is equally important to have the right infrastructure that provides necessary automation to keep pace with application innovation and IT service delivery. HPE will be there as your partner as you take the journey towards a better enterprise that will quickly turn Ideas into successful business models.
